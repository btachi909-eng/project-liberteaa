About Us

The coffee shop, 2 Shot Espresso, is owned by Paul Stephen Constantino Berico and is located at 1735 Velasquez St. Metro Manila. With the consent of the owner, both the name and photos of the store may be included in this research. The shop has been operating since 2022 and was established with the straightforward goal of offering good coffee to its customers. The vision behind the business is to provide drinks that meet high standards in both quality and taste, making coffee more enjoyable and accessible to anyone who visits.

Our mission is to offer a wide range of high-quality products at competitive prices, along with exceptional customer service.

Thank you for choosing us for your shopping needs. We look forward to serving you!
